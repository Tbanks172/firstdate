📦 What's Inside
```

155 questions. 10 categories. Each one designed to go somewhere real.

01 — Money & Ambition (20 questions)
What they truly believe about wealth, work, and what financial independence actually looks like to them — not the aspirational answer, the honest one. Do they separate their self-worth from their net worth? Would they work a job they hate for three years to fund something they love? What's the smartest financial decision they've made so far?
02 — Identity & Psychology (20 questions)
What drives them, what stops them, and what their inner voice sounds like at their lowest. What they've had to consciously unlearn from their upbringing. Whether they're living their own life — or someone else's expectations. What their relationship with failure actually looks like.
03 — Lifestyle & Aesthetics (15 questions)
Who they really are when no one's watching. How they spend their Saturdays, what they own that genuinely makes their life better, whether they have a complicated relationship with rest — and the big one: is the life they're building for themselves, or for people watching?
04 — Society & Systems (15 questions)
What they actually think about the world they grew up in — their culture, their country, community responsibility. Whether they feel patriotism is still relevant. What they'd fix first if they had real power. The conversations most people avoid entirely on a first date.
05 — Purpose & Direction (15 questions)
Did they choose their path deliberately — or fall into it? What would they regret not trying by 50? Is work-life balance real to them, or just what people say when they're burning out? Is there a version of success in their field that actually excites them?
06 — Relationships, Values & Depth (15 questions)
What they believe about love, loyalty, intimacy, and vulnerability. Whether love is a feeling or a decision. What they need in a relationship that they've never quite known how to ask for. The most important lesson a past relationship taught them about themselves.
07 — Feminism & Womanhood (15 questions)
Honest, nuanced, and unguarded conversations about strength, expectation, financial independence, and sisterhood. What society still tells women to shrink that they refuse to. Whether "having it all" is empowering or an exhausting trap. Real conversations, not performances.
08 — Masculinity & What It Actually Means (15 questions)
What men were taught to suppress — and what real strength actually looks like. Whether masculinity is in crisis or just being redefined. Whether vulnerability in a man is attractive or still makes people uncomfortable, honestly. The conversations most men have never had out loud.
09 — False Masculinity & False Feminism (10 questions)
The questions that cut through performance — where real growth ends and armour begins. The difference between setting standards and just being unavailable. Whether "alpha male" culture is confidence or insecurity in a louder outfit. Where the language lies.
10 — Parenting & How We Were Raised (15 questions)
The most revealing category in the deck. Because how someone was loved as a child shapes everything about how they love as an adult. Whether they want children — and if that answer is truly theirs. Whether it's possible to break generational cycles, or if they just show up differently.
```
